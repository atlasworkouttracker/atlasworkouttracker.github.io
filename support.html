Common topics

Installation & access

Atlas Workout Tracker is currently in limited release. Access is granted via an unlisted Google Workspace Marketplace listing — email the address above to request the install link.

Sign-in / authentication issues

The most common cause of the dialog failing to load is being signed into multiple Google accounts in the same browser. The add-on opens under one account but its background calls fall through to a different account. Workarounds:

  • Use Chrome's Guest profile (no extensions, no other accounts) to verify the issue is multi-account-related.
  • Sign out of all other Google accounts in your browser, leave only the one you installed the add-on under.
  • Try a different browser if the above doesn't help.

Undo time limit

The "Undo last week creation" action restores the previous week within 24 hours of creating a new week. After that window, use Google Sheets' built-in version history (File → Version history → See version history) to restore an earlier state of the sheet.

Permissions / scopes

The add-on requests four narrow OAuth scopes — spreadsheets.currentonly (per-file sheet access), drive.file (per-file Drive access), script.container.ui (display the dialog/sidebar), and script.locale (read your locale for date/time formatting). The broader drive or spreadsheets scopes are NOT requested. Read the privacy policy for details.

Uninstall

Remove the add-on at any time from your Google Workspace add-ons settings. This revokes all OAuth grants and prevents the add-on from accessing your sheets.
