Atlas Workout Tracker

Plan and track resistance training in your Google Sheet.

What it is

Atlas Workout Tracker is a Google Workspace add-on that turns a Google Sheet into a structured resistance-training tracker. It builds out each week from publisher-controlled templates, finalizes completed weeks automatically, and lets you configure the next week from a friendly dialog instead of editing rows by hand.

What it does

  • Configure next week — opens a dialog where you pick RT days, mesocycle, day templates, and advanced settings (units, timezone, bodyweight). Builds the new week and finalizes the previous week in one step.
  • Undo last week creation — reverses the most recent week creation within 24 hours, restoring the previous week to its pre-finalize state.
  • Initialize tracker — first-time setup for a fresh sheet.

Privacy stance

Everything runs inside your own Google account. The add-on does not operate any servers, does not collect analytics, and does not transmit your workout data anywhere. Read the privacy policy for details.
