Draft Tester Opt-Out

What it means to be a draft tester

Atlas Workout Tracker is a Google Workspace add-on currently undergoing review for full Marketplace publication. While the review is in progress, a small number of draft testers have been added by the developer so they can install and use the pre-release version of the add-on. Draft testers are typically friends or early users who agreed to try the add-on ahead of public release.

How to opt out

If you have been added as a draft tester and no longer wish to be one, contact the developer at aetherlabs21@gmail.com with the subject line “Draft tester opt-out”. You will be removed from the draft tester list within a few days. No reason is required.

Removing yourself from the draft tester list does not uninstall the add-on from your Google account. To also uninstall:

  1. Open https://myaccount.google.com/permissions
  2. Find “Atlas Workout Tracker” in the list
  3. Click Remove access

What data we keep about you

The developer retains only the email address you provided when you were added as a tester (used to grant pre-release access via the Google Cloud test-user list). Once you opt out, your email is removed from that list. The add-on itself does not store any personal data about you on any external server — see the Privacy Policy for full details on data handling.
